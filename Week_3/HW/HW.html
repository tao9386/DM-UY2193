3. Recipe Instruction

  1. Prepare the tofu: Slice the tofu into pieces that are easy to eat.
  2. Cook the spices: Heat oil in a skillet and add the spicy broad bean paste, ginger, and garlic, stirring until they release a rich aroma.
  3. Incorporate the ground meat: Add the ground meat to the skillet, frying it until it changes color.
  4. Introduce the tofu: Place the tofu chunks into the skillet, mixing gently to avoid crumbling the pieces.
  5. Flavoring: Pour a suitable quantity of water into the skillet, adding Sichuan peppercorns, soy sauce, salt, and other spices to taste.
  6. Thickening: Near the end of the cooking process, blend in a cornstarch-water mixture to give the sauce a thicker consistency.
  7. Serving: Spoon the finished Mapo Tofu into a serving dish, topped with a sprinkling of scallions and a splash of chili oil for that perfect finish.
